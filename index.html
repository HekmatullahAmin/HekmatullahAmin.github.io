About Me

Building software that people actually enjoy using.

I'm a Kotlin Multiplatform and Android Developer passionate about building seamless, modern, and scalable experiences across platforms. I specialize in Kotlin Multiplatform, Compose Multiplatform, Jetpack Compose, Java, MySQL, Git, Firebase, CI/CD, and the full end-to-end app lifecycle across Google Play Store, App Store, and Mac App Store.

Over my journey, I've:

  • Migrated financial modules to Compose and Kotlin Multiplatform, improving performance, maintainability, and code reuse across platforms.
  • Built CI/CD pipelines using GitHub Actions to automate app releases to TestFlight, Firebase, Google Play Store, App Store, and Mac App Store, streamlining internal testing and production deployments.
  • Reviewed code and mentored contributors, helping them grow while ensuring high-quality code and timely delivery.

I'm driven by continuous learning — new tools, new languages, and new ways to solve problems. I love challenges that push me to explore, read, experiment, and grow. And nothing feels better than helping others succeed along the way.

I'm currently seeking opportunities in Software Engineering, Android Development, or Kotlin Multiplatform Development, where I can contribute, learn, and collaborate with great teams.

Outside of work, I'm a huge sports enthusiast. Whether it's Football, Chess, Table Tennis, Volleyball, Tennis, or Cricket — I'm always in, often competing, and proudly winning trophies, medals, and certificates.
